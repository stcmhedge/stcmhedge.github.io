Our Strategy
STCM was founded in the midst of one of the greatest economic crises in modern times. We engage in robust tail-hedging strategies to minimize tail risk and create a portfolio that benefits from unpredictable events. We are primarily long-short equity and pursue many different strategies to learn more about investing, including global macro and and event-driven strategies.
